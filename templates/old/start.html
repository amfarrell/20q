Welcome to the Open Mind User Test!

People constantly rely on unspoken assumptions to communicate and make sense of the world. Computers don't have these assumptions, so they often appear stupid. Open Mind is a project aiming to collect basic statements of common sense knowledge in order to make computer interfaces better.

We're testing some new interfaces for adding knowledge to Open Mind. Once you click "Begin", you're going to try one of these interfaces. We'd like you to use it as efficiently as you can and give us some feedback.

(Don't worry too much about grammar. We're not currently testing Open Mind's ability to put together a correct sentence.)
